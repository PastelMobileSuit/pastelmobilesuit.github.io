Yugen

Yugen is a 3D exploration-based horror adventure in which you must solve the mystery of what happened in an abandoned Japanese village.

Collect clues about the fate of the village and its inhabitants, and combine them to gain new information.

Avoid horrifying monsters against which you have no defense.

Maintain your sanity, or suffer the consequences.

Play Yugen Now

Yugen was the first full 3D game I worked on, and was made as the final project for a Game Design course at the University of Portland. The game was written in Unity, and was created alongside Hailee Kenney, Sam Golloway, Stan Peck, and Janel Raab. I served as writer, a programmer responsible for aspects such as the sanity mechanics, and the head designer.

Music: 'Himalayan Atmosphere' by Kevin MacLeod (Incompetech.com)
