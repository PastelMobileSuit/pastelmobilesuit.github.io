Dark Dungeon

Dark Dungeon is a top-down, 2D stealth-puzzler dungeon crawl where you must steal treasure from guards.

Avoid the guards by switching into Dark mode...

But watch out for the ghosts that inhabit it!

Play Dark Dungeon Now

Dark Dungeon was the first game I completed, and was made for a Game Design class at the University of Portland. The game was written in C# using the XNA framework, and was created alongside Hailee Kenney and Justice Nichols. I served as head designer and did a large portion of the programming.

All art (minus the font) was created by Hailee Kenney.

Music: 'Video Dungeon Crawl' by Kevin MacLeod (Incompetech.com)
