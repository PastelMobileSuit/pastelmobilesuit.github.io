Eternity Engine

Eternity Engine is a story-driven 2D shmup RPG hybrid, in which you fight in battles as the hero, Blake Slate.

Experience an in-depth story about the fight against an evil AI

Purchase gems with money earned from battles and equip them to power up your character

Play Eternity Engine Now

Eternity Engine was created as a year-long Senior Design project at the University of Portland. The game was written in ActionScript 3 using the Flixel library alongside Aaron Dobbe, Stan Peck, and Bobby Stiles. I served primarily as a programmer.

Art by Marlowe Dobbe.

Music: 'Cephalopod', 'Legend of One', 'Mechanolith', 'Rocket', 'Steel Rods', and 'The Complex' by Kevin MacLeod (Incompetech.com)
