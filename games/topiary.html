topiary!

topiary! is a 2D side-scrolling hedge-'em-up created for Global Game Jam 2015.

Play as Gale as she trims hedges in her garden, but be careful not to trim them too much!

And watch out, things may not be what they seem...

Play topiary! Now

topiary! was created in 48 hours for Global Game Jam 2015, and was developed in Unity using its native 2D tools. It was written in C# and was developed alongside Christian Scandariato, Erik Sundberg, and Kayli Edwards. I served as one of the chief programmers and a designer.

Art by Erik Sundberg and Kayli Edwards (www.kayliedwards.com)
